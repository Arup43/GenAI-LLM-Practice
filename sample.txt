Paris is the capital of France. 
The Eiffel Tower is the most iconic landmark in Paris.
The Louvre Museum houses the Mona Lisa.
The Seine River flows through the city.
The Notre-Dame Cathedral is a famous Gothic cathedral.
The Arc de Triomphe is a monumental archway.
The Champs-Élysées is a famous avenue.
The Palace of Versailles is a royal palace.
The Montmartre district is known for its artistic history.
The Latin Quarter is a historic area with many cafes and bookstores.
